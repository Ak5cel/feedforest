About FeedForest

FeedForest makes newsfeed subscription a breeze! Instead of having to subscribe to several newsletters, get the latest updates from your favourite sites with a single account.

Features include:

  • A database of articles updated every hour.
  • A customisable Feed Wall with the latest updates from your selected feeds.
  • Option to get daily emails at your preferred time with the same contents as your personal feed wall.

Built with Flask
